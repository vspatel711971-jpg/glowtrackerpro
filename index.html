📖 Cycle Phases Explained
🩸
Menstrual (Days 1–5)
Uterine lining sheds. Rest, warmth and iron-rich food is key.
🌱
Follicular (Days 6–13)
Estrogen rises. Energy and creativity peak — great for new habits!
🌸
Ovulation (Days 14–16)
LH surge releases egg. Skin glows naturally. Fertile window is ~5 days before + 1 day after ovulation.
🌙
Luteal (Days 17–28)
Progesterone rises. PMS possible — support with magnesium, less sugar, extra self-care.
